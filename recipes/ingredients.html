Lomo Saltado

Ingredients

Serves 2-3

Main Course:

  • Tender Beef, 1 lb
  • Red Onion, 1 sliced thick
  • Cilantro, a few springs, chopped
  • Soy Sauce, 1/4 cup
  • Garlic, 1 cloves minced
  • Wine wine, 1/4 Cup
  • Red Wine Vinegar, 1 Tbsp
  • Aji Amarillo: Is there such thing as too much?
  • Tomatoes, 1 sliced thick
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • Steak Fries
  • White Rice, 2 cups

Accompaniments:

  • White Rice
  • French Fries
