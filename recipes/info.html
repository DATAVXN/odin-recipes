Lomo Saltado

What is Lomo Saltado?

It's a Chinese-Peruvian stir fry dish made with beef filet considered part of Peru's "Chifa" fusion gastronomy. It's one of Peru's primary and most popular dishes both nationally and internationally. It's rich flavor and robust ingredient list is sure to quench your hunger.


Where did it come from?

  • Born in Peru in the 1800's
  • Born to Peruvian Chinese Colonies
  • Relatively unknown in US during 1980's
  • Achieved worldwide recognition 2000's and beyond
  • Available in capital cities across US.
